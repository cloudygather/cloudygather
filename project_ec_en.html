Overview

This project is main for e-commerce on mobile phone and backstage on desktop, offers the service to stores build up their own brand store online and broaden their business through digital marketing, we build up the brand like shop equation and lives of select as example. In this project, I major in UIUX, set up the interaction between user interface and users, guildlines, components and prototypes for visual style by the requirement of functions.

Brand

Case1.Stylish book store
Through the ec system to integrate the traditional orders into system, synchronize sells stationeries, tickets, etc. System offers stores the functions about products management and build up the coupon.

Case2.Lives of select
Stores sell merchandise of multiple brands throgh the ec system on mobile device, offer the information on sale and QA for customers.
